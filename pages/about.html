How did it start?
Thanks to a member of the Open Source community, Fukubi, he was able to identify that he needed a place where all projects at different levels could be found and shared.

Anderson "Yagasaki" Marlon got his hands dirty and developed the whole idea, structure and on October 13, he founded NERV Open Source, a Brazilian community at an international level to bring together projects of different levels and recognitions.
